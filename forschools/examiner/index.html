BayScope Examiner Features

BayScope Examiner offers a robust platform for creating, managing, and evaluating online examinations with ease, providing a seamless experience for educators, course creators, educational bodies, companies, and organizations.

Dynamic Exam Creation

Create exams with dynamic content, including multimedia support, and a variety of question types.
