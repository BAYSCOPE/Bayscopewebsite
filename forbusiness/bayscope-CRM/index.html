Manual integration (Developers/existing site owners)

Integrating BayScope into an existing site requires a few simple steps. Developers can easily add our CRM functionalities to enhance their platforms.

CRM Initialization

After adding the integration script to your site, initialize the CRM with your unique key provided by BayScope:

// <script src="https://crm.your-business.com/integrate.js" ></script>
<script>
window.onload = function() {
CRM.init('YOUR_UNIQUE_KEY');
};
</script>

Replace 'YOUR_UNIQUE_KEY' with the key provided to you. This will activate the CRM functionalities on your website. Access your CRM dashboard on crm.your-business.com
